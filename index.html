We want to design an application which is going to be supporting the physical and mental health and wellness of the elderly population. The application is going to be designed based on elderly people living at home or in a nursing home. There are many challenges that the senior population faces, such as loneliness, physical inactivity, mental health issues, etc. For these reasons, we have decided to develop Carefree, an online platform that englobes various modules. We want to create an application which is going to be entertaining, keep them in touch with the community, as well as adapt to their lifestyle
